About

The Sri Lanka Planetarium offers educational shows about the cosmos. Learn about stars, planets, and galaxies in an immersive environment.

Show Times

Show times: 10:00 A.M. and 02:00 P.M.

Weekdays: Reserved for school/institute shows only.

Saturdays: Open for the general public. Please arrive at least half an hour before your show time.

Duration: 1 hour and 30 minutes.

Closed on Sundays, Mondays, and public holidays.

For seat reservation for school shows, email: slp.booking.lk@gmail.com

For more details, call: (+94) 11 2586 499

Ticket Prices

Weekdays: Students - Rs. 50, Adults - Rs. 150

Weekends (Only for students in school uniforms and children under 12 years): Students - Rs. 100, Adults - Rs. 250
