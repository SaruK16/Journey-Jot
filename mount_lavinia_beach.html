About

Mount Lavinia Beach, nestled along Dehiwela, Colombo, boasts golden sands, captivating sunsets, and a rich history entwined with colonial romance. Popular for swimming, water sports, and sunset parties, it offers a blend of relaxation, recreation, and culinary delights with its array of beachside eateries. Accessible and picturesque, it's a top destination for locals and tourists alike, inviting all to soak in its scenic beauty and vibrant atmosphere.

Open Hours

Mount Lavinia Beach doesn't have specific "opening hours" as it's a public beach accessible at all times.

However, it's worth noting that certain facilities like beachside restaurants, cafes, and shops may have their own operating hours. These establishments typically open during the day and may stay open until late evening, especially during peak tourist seasons or weekends when there's more activity along the beach. Overall, visitors can enjoy Mount Lavinia Beach throughout the day and into the evening, but specific amenities may have varying hours of operation.

Ticket Prices

There are typically no tickets or entry fees required to access Mount Lavinia Beach itself, as it's a public beach open to everyone.
