About

Colombo City Centre is a 47-storey mixed-use development. Colombo City Centre comprises a five storey retail space which consists of 3,675 m2 (39,560 sq ft), including a 164-room hotel and 192 residential apartments.

Open Hours

Typically, there is no entrance fee to access the mall or its facilities..

Visitors can enter the mall free of charge.

The mall is usually open seven days a week, including weekends and public holidays.
