About

The Port City refers to a significant urban development project in Colombo, Sri Lanka, known officially as the Colombo International Financial City (CIFC) or the Colombo Port City. It is an ambitious initiative aimed at transforming a reclaimed land area into a modern metropolis, featuring state-of-the-art infrastructure, commercial facilities, residential complexes, and recreational spaces.
